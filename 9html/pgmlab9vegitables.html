Vegetables are rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Eating a variety of vegetables daily helps maintain health and prevent chronic diseases.Vegetables are the edible parts of plants, such as leaves, stems, roots, and flowers, that are important for a healthy diet. They are typically low in calories but high in essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. Eating a variety of vegetables can help protect against chronic illnesses like heart disease and diabetes.
